# Ars Nouveau

The Ars Nouveau chapter begins after the final Actually Additions milestone and introduces programmable spellcraft through a practical magical workshop.

## Foundations

The first 21 quests cover:

- The Worn Notebook and Archwood gathering
- Source Gems and the Novice Spell Book
- Scribe's Table glyph inscription
- Spell forms, effects, and first spell composition
- Imbuement Chamber and Source Jars
- Agronomic and optional Volcanic Sourcelinks
- Source Relays and Arcane Pedestals
- Magebloom cultivation
- Enchanting Apparatus construction
- Magical essence crafting
- Optional spell automation and mage equipment

The milestone **An Arcane Workshop** confirms that the player can generate Source, compose spells, and use the core crafting infrastructure required by later Ars Nouveau progression.
