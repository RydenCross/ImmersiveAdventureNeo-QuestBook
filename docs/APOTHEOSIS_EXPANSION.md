# Apotheosis Expansion

This optional post-mastery branch begins after **Apotheosis Mastery** and adds 20 quests focused on mythic boss hunting, high-quality gem refinement, specialized reforging, advanced enchanting infrastructure, multiple finished equipment builds, and difficult field trials.

The original **Apotheosis Mastery** quest remains the downstream handoff for Applied Energistics 2 and Endgame. Players can therefore continue normal progression without completing the expansion.
