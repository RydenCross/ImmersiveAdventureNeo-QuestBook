# Applied Energistics 2 Expansion

This optional post-mastery branch extends **Master of the ME Network** into endgame digital infrastructure.

The expansion covers:

- 64k bulk item storage and dedicated drive organization
- Overflow protection and stock control
- Larger crafting CPUs, parallel co-processing, and a twenty-pattern library
- Complex multi-stage autocrafting jobs
- Redundant channel paths and network monitoring
- Quantum Network Bridges and remote industrial sites
- Spatial IO testing and safe spatial-storage procedures
- Failure-recovery drills and a final quantum-network milestone

All twenty quests are optional. Mekanism and Endgame continue to unlock from **Master of the ME Network**, preserving the established progression path.
