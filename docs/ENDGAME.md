# Endgame Chapter

The Endgame chapter contains 24 quests that combine the major progression paths into one final phase.

## Progression goals

- Require completion of Create, Actually Additions, Ars Nouveau, Apotheosis, AE2, and Mekanism.
- Build a monitored, failure-tolerant command center and power backbone.
- Connect autocrafting, stock control, remote logistics, and cross-mod processing.
- Prove advanced spellcraft and an optimized equipment loadout in the field.
- Validate reactor reliability, energy reserves, and ME network scale.
- Complete Wither, beacon, and Ender Dragon milestones.
- Finish with automation and adventure mastery objectives.

Many endgame tasks use checkmarks because they evaluate complete systems and player-built designs rather than a single item ID.
