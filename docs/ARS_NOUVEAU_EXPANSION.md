# Ars Nouveau Expansion

Commit 33 adds twenty optional quests after **Master of Practical Spellcraft**.
The branch develops an archmage-scale workshop without changing the milestone
used to unlock Apotheosis or Endgame.

## Themes

- Diverse and buffered Source generation
- Managed relay networks and remote magical outposts
- Permanent ritual sites and magical transport
- Familiar specialization
- Safe spell-turret networks
- Enchanting Apparatus automation and essence reserves
- A documented spell archive
- Unattended workshop reliability

Every quest in this expansion is optional. Players can pursue the branch for
long-term magical mastery without blocking the main questbook progression.
