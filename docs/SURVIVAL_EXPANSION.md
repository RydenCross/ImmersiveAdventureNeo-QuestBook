# Survival Expansion

Commit 30 adds twenty optional post-homestead quests to the Survival chapter.

The new branches deepen early-game play without delaying Mining or Exploration:

- diverse crops: potatoes, carrots, beetroot, pumpkins, and melons;
- fishing and cooked seafood;
- safe beekeeping, honey, and a managed apiary;
- a complete vanilla utility workshop.

All expansion quests unlock after **A Proper Homestead** and remain optional.
