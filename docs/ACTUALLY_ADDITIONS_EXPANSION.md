# Actually Additions Expansion

This optional post-completion branch extends **Actually Equipped** with twenty late-game workshop objectives.

The expansion focuses on four themes:

- diversified renewable generation and reserve energy storage;
- structured Energy Laser Relay distribution;
- automated Empowerer and Atomic Reconstructor production;
- filtered collection, phantom interfaces, and unattended operation.

Every quest is optional. The original **Actually Equipped** milestone remains the dependency used by Ars Nouveau and later chapters, so the expansion does not block the established progression path.
