# Survival Chapter

The generated Survival chapter contains 25 quests and unlocks after the final Welcome quest.

Its progression covers:

- storage and organization
- shields, armor, and ranged defense
- renewable crops and animal husbandry
- cooking and utility blocks
- water, sugar cane, leather, and books
- renewable timber, charcoal, and base lighting
- navigation tools
- a final homestead milestone

Optional side quests are marked in the source definitions without blocking the chapter finale.
