# Actually Additions

The Actually Additions chapter begins after Create mastery and introduces Forge Energy through a compact, guided workshop.

## Foundations

The first 21 quests cover:

- Black Quartz and the in-game manual
- Coal-powered Forge Energy generation
- Batteries and the Battery Box
- Atomic Reconstructor operation
- Restonia, Enori, Void, Palis, Diamatine, and Emeradic crystals
- Laser Wrench and Energy Laser Relays
- Energizer and Enervator
- Display Stands and the Empowerer

The final milestone, **A Reconstructed Workshop**, unlocks later Actually Additions processing, farming, oil, tools, and utility progression.


## Machines and Resources

The next 19 quests cover:

- Crusher, Double Crusher, and Powered Furnace processing
- Farmer automation and renewable Canola cultivation
- Canola Press, Fermenting Barrel, and Oil Generator power
- Storage Crates and the Item Interface
- Breaker and Placer block automation
- Fluid Collector and Fluid Placer utility systems

The milestone **An Actually Automated Workshop** confirms that the player can operate a self-sustaining powered workshop.

## Tools and Utilities

The third progression section adds 18 quests focused on equipment and remote utility systems:

- A rechargeable, augmentable Drill with speed, area, Fortune, and Silk Touch paths
- Wings of the Bats as an optional mobility objective
- Player Interface and Phantom remote-access devices
- Ranged Collector and Item Filter automation
- Teleport Staff, Handheld Filler, and optional Coffee Machine utility quests
- The final **Actually Equipped** chapter milestone

This section begins after **An Actually Automated Workshop** and becomes the new completion point for the Actually Additions chapter.
