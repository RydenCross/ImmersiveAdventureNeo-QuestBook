# Challenges Chapter

The Challenges chapter contains optional long-term objectives unlocked after the Endgame completion quest.

Its 30 quests are organized around six themes:

- resource stockpiles and large-scale storage;
- megafactories, autocrafting, and remote industry;
- power generation, reserves, reactor stress tests, and recovery;
- Ars Nouveau spellcraft and Apotheosis equipment mastery;
- exploration, bosses, museums, and world documentation;
- city building, transit, reliability, and completionism.

Most objectives use checkmark tasks because they evaluate player-built systems or accomplishments rather than a single item. Concrete trophy and stockpile objectives use item tasks where reliable item identifiers exist.
